ABOUT US

Welcome to MAPA-ALERTO! A project dedicated to ensuring the safety and preparedness of the COECSA building in LPU - Cavite. Our initiative focuses on developing a comprehensive Evacuation Thematic Map for the COECSA building, designed to provide clear and accessible emergency guidance.
