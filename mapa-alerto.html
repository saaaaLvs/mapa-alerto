MAPA-ALERTO

Welcome to MAPA-ALERTO, a project dedicated to ensuring the safety and preparedness of Lyceans. Our initiative focuses on developing a comprehensive Evacuation Thematic Map for the COECSA building, designed to provide clear and accessible emergency guidance.

Through this project, we aim to enhance safety awareness by equipping Lyceans with the necessary knowledge to respond to future emergencies. By integrating detailed evacuation routes and safety ptocols, we strive to create a secure and well-prepared environment within the COECSA community.

At MAPA-ALERTO, safety is our priority. Our mission is to empower individuals with essential emergency preparedness tools, ensuring that everyone can navigate critical situations with confidence.
