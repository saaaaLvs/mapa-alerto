Frequently
Asked Question

The Security Office is located on the ground floor of the COECSA building at Lyceum of the Philippines University - Cavite. When facing the front entrance of COECSA, you’ll find the office on the left side. Enter the building and turn left—it's just a short walk down the hallway. The office is marked and usually has security personnel nearby, so it’s easy to spot if you need help.

You can find security officers on every floor of the COECSA building, typically stationed at the central area or near stairwells and hallways. They are positioned strategically to assist students, faculty, and visitors, and to ensure safety throughout the building. If you need help, just look for a uniformed officer nearby.

During emergencies, Lyceans should contact either the school’s official hotlines or the appropriate external emergency services. For quick reference and specific contact numbers, please refer to the Emergency Guide > Contacts provided by the university. It's recommended to keep a copy of this guide accessible at all times.

The purpose of the MAPA-ALERTO site is to help Lyceans quickly and efficiently navigate the safest routes during emergencies within the COECSA building. It serves as a visual guide that highlights evacuation paths, safety zones, and important emergency points, ensuring that students and staff can respond swiftly and safely in times of crisis.

Yes, the map is accurate and officially approved by the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP). It has been carefully reviewed to ensure it meets all current safety standards and guidelines, making it a reliable reference for emergency preparedness and evacuation planning.
