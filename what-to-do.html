What To Do
during Emergency

1. Listen to Instructions

• Follow your teacher or staff directions immediately.

• Don’t panic or shout – stay quiet so you can hear updates.

2. Emergency Types

• Fire: Evacuate quickly, don’t use elevators, stay low if there’s smoke.

• Lockdown: Hide, lock doors, silence your phone, stay quiet.

• Earthquake: Drop, Cover, and Hold under sturdy furniture.

• Weather Emergency: Move to designated safe areas.

3. Stick With Your Group

• Stay with your class or assigned buddy.

• Don’t wander off or try to go home without permission.

4. Stay Off Your Phone (Unless Necessary)

• Use your phone only to contact emergency services or if instructed.

• Networks may be overloaded.

5. Know the Exits and Safe Zones

• Memorize evacuation routes and meeting points.

• Know where emergency kits and alarms are.

6. Help If You Can

• If someone is hurt or confused, help them stay calm or notify an adult.

7. Report Anything Suspicious

• Tell a teacher or staff member if you see something unusual or dangerous.
